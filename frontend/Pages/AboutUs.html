About us

RAKN is an innovative AI-powered parking violation management system designed to enhance parking enforcement efficiency and organization. The system enables security personnel to detect, report, and manage vehicle violations seamlessly using advanced technology.

With RAKN, violations are accurately logged, and security teams can efficiently track and address parking infractions. The platform also integrates real-time data analysis, automated reporting, and a user-friendly interface, ensuring a smooth and transparent process.
